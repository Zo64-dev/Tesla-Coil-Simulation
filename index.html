Why learn by playing?

Turn the knobs yourself

A formula says voltage scales the field. A slider lets you feel it. When you find resonance by hand, you never forget what resonance means.

💥

Detune the coil. Overdrive the voltage. Nothing sparks, melts, or bills you for equipment. Every wrong setting teaches you where the right one lives.

🔬

Real numbers, real physics

These aren't cartoons. The labs use genuine constants — real ionization energies, real inverse-square falloff — so intuition you build here transfers to the classroom.
